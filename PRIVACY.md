# Privacy Policy

Kanji Trainer is a local-first Android app.

## Data Collection

The app does not collect, transmit, sell, or share personal data.

## Network Access

The app does not require a network connection for its core study features.

## Local Data

Study progress, review history, and optional mnemonic notes are stored locally on
the device in the app database.

The app enables Android Auto Backup, so this data may be backed up to your own
Google account and restored automatically when you reinstall the app or set up a
new device. This is handled entirely by Android and your Google account settings;
the developer has no access to it. You can turn it off in your device's backup
settings.

## Third Parties

The app does not use analytics SDKs, advertising SDKs, or third-party tracking
services.

## Contact

For questions, open an issue in the GitHub repository.
